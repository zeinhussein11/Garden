The Garden Project is more than just a website – it is a digital celebration of nature’elegance and serenity. We built this site to show how HTML and CSS can bring beauty to the web just like flowers do in real life.

Inspired by the soothing colors and calmness of nature, our garden aims to provide a peaceful online space. Here, you can explore various types of flowers, understand their meaning, and enjoy a relaxing digital experience.

Created with passion by [Zakaria], this project is part of a mission to combine creativity, nature, and technology into one smart and simple space. Whether you're a plant lover or a beginner in web development, this garden is made for you.

Our garden is a shared and peaceful space where everyone is welcome. To keep it clean and beautiful, we follow a few simple rules that help us work together in harmony.

1 we respect the plants and flowers. No one should pick the flowers or damage any part of the garden. Every plant has its purpose and adds to the beauty of nature.

2 always keep the garden clean. Do not throw trash on the ground. Use the waste bins provided, and help us keep the area fresh and green.

3 water the plants gently and only when needed. Over-watering can damage the roots, and under-watering can dry the plants. Balance is key in caring for nature.

4 we do not allow loud noise or playing music with high volume. The garden is a quiet place where people come to relax, read, or reflect in peace.

5 children are welcome, but they must be guided by adults. Running or playing roughly in the garden is not allowed as it may harm the plants and disturb others.

6 tools and equipment should be used carefully and returned to their place after use. Everyone shares responsibility for keeping the garden organized.

7 be kind and helpful to others. Smile, greet fellow gardeners, and share knowledge. Together, we make the garden a better place for all of us.
